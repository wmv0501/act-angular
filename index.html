Welcome to Advanced Care Training School

Advanced Care Training School(ACT) is a State Approved Training Facility. Certified by the Department of Human Services for teaching Certified Nurse Assistant Training and CNA Recertification. Offering 100 hours CNA and 24 hours recertification courses. Spouse Education and Career Opportunity Approved (SECO), formerly known as MYCAA.
